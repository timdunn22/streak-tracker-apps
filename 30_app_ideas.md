## 1. QuitLit — Smoking Quit Counter

- **Core Concept:** A dead-simple quit-smoking tracker with a money-saved counter, health milestones, and daily streak.
- **Target User:** Smokers trying to quit (ages 20–45).
- **Pain It Solves:** No simple, motivating way to track smoke-free progress and see tangible money saved.
- **Why They'll Pay Monthly ($7.99/mo):** Unlock detailed health recovery timeline, widget, and shareable milestone cards.
- **Why It Can Go Viral on TikTok:** "Day 42 of no cigarettes — I've saved $630" posts. Before/after transformation content. Creators film their streak milestones.
- **Simple MVP Features:** Quit date picker, day counter, money-saved calculator, health milestone timeline, streak badge, share card generator.
- **Monetization Structure:** Free 7-day trial, then $7.99/mo or $49.99/yr via Superwall.
- **Paywall Hooks:** Free users see day count only. Paid unlocks money tracker, health timeline, widgets, and custom share cards.
- **Why It Wins Through Simplicity:** One screen. One counter. One clear goal. No community, no chat, no complexity.
- **Build Difficulty:** Very Easy
