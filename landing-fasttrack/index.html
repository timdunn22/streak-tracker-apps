Frequently Asked Questions

Is FastTrack really free?

Yes. No subscription, no premium tier. Every feature is free. Unlike Simple or DoFasting, we will never charge you.

How is this different from other fasting apps?

Most fasting apps charge $10-20/month and require accounts. FastTrack is 100% free, requires no account, and stores data only on your device.

Does it track my fasting window?

FastTrack tracks your consecutive days of successful fasting. It's a consistency tracker — count each day you complete your fasting protocol.

I broke my fast early. What do I do?

If you completed most of your fasting window, keep the streak. If you fully broke protocol, use the reset — your history is preserved.

What is autophagy?

Autophagy is your body's cellular cleanup process, activated during fasting. Damaged cells are recycled and repaired. It typically increases significantly after 16-24 hours of fasting.
