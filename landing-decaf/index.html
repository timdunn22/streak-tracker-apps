Frequently Asked Questions

Is Decaf really free?

Yes. Completely free. No subscription, no ads, no premium features.

How long do withdrawal headaches last?

Typically 2-9 days, with peak intensity around day 1-2. Stay hydrated — it helps significantly.

Will I have energy without caffeine?

Yes. After 2-4 weeks, most people report more stable, consistent energy than they had with caffeine. The spikes and crashes disappear.

Does this include tea?

That's your choice. Some people quit all caffeine (coffee, tea, energy drinks), others just reduce. Decaf tracks whatever protocol you follow.

I had a coffee. Is my progress lost?

Reset your streak if you want to start fresh — previous days are always saved. Or keep going if it was a one-time slip.
