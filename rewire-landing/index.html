Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rewire.

Is Rewire really free?
Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no paywall, no "free trial." Every feature is available to everyone.
How do I install it on my phone?
Open rewire-psi.vercel.app in your phone's browser. On iOS, tap Share then "Add to Home Screen." On Android, tap the three dots menu and "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App." It works like a native app after that.
Can anyone see my data?
No. Your streak data is stored only in your browser's local storage on your device. No data is transmitted to any server. We have no database, no user accounts, and no way to see your information.
What happens if I clear my browser data?
Your streak data will be lost since it's stored locally. To protect against this, install Rewire as a PWA (add to home screen) — this gives it its own storage space separate from your browser.
What is the 90-day reboot?
Research suggests it takes roughly 90 days for the brain to significantly rewire neural pathways associated with compulsive behavior. This is why Rewire tracks your progress toward 90 days as the primary goal, though recovery continues beyond that.
I relapsed. Is my progress lost?
No. Rewire has a compassionate reset feature that saves your previous streak to your history and calculates your total clean days. Every day counts, even across multiple streaks. Setbacks are part of recovery.
Does it work on desktop?
Yes. Rewire works on any device with a modern web browser — phone, tablet, or desktop computer.
