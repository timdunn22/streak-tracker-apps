Science-Backed Recovery Phases

Your brain goes through real neurological changes during recovery. Rewire shows you exactly where you are.

Day 0-3

Withdrawal

Peak cravings. Your brain is demanding the dopamine it's used to.
Day 3-7

Adjusting

Cravings ease. Brain fog is common as dopamine receptors start resensitizing.
Day 7-14

Recovering

Energy increases. Sleep improves. You start feeling more present.
Day 14-30

Resetting

Neural pathways are actively reshaping. Confidence builds. Flatlining may occur.
Day 30-60

Strengthening

New habits solidify. Willpower feels natural, not forced.
Day 60-90

Rewiring

Deep prefrontal cortex changes. Urges become rare and weak.
Day 90+

Rewired

Your brain has fundamentally changed. This is your new normal.
