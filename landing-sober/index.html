Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sober really free?

Yes. No subscription, no paywall. Unlike I Am Sober, every feature is free with no upsells.

Is this app private?

Completely. No account, no email, no server. All data stays on your device. Nobody can see your sobriety status.

Should I see a doctor before quitting?

If you drink heavily, yes. Alcohol withdrawal can be medically serious. This app is a tracking tool, not medical advice.

I drank. Is my progress gone?

No. Sober saves your previous streak and tracks total sober days. Relapse is part of recovery for many people.

How does this compare to I Am Sober?

I Am Sober requires an account and charges for premium features. Sober is completely free, requires no account, and keeps all data on your device.
