Frequently Asked Questions

Is VapeFree really free?
Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no paywall, no upsells. Every feature is available to everyone.
How do I install it on my phone?
Open the app in your browser. On iOS, tap Share then "Add to Home Screen." On Android, tap the menu and "Install App." It works like a native app.
Can anyone see my vaping data?
No. All data stays in your browser's local storage on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. We have zero access to your information.
What happens when I get a craving?
Open VapeFree and look at your streak. See how far you've come. Read the daily quote. The craving typically passes in 3-5 minutes.
I vaped again. Is my progress lost?
No. VapeFree saves your previous streak and counts total vape-free days across all attempts. Every day counts.
