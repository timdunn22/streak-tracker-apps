Frequently Asked Questions

Is SugarFree really free?

Yes. No subscription, no ads, no premium tier. Every feature is free forever.

Does this mean no fruit?

That's up to you. Many people quit added/refined sugar while keeping whole fruits. SugarFree tracks whatever protocol you choose.

How long until cravings stop?

Most people report cravings peaking at day 3-5 and significantly decreasing by day 14-21. Everyone is different.

I ate sugar. What now?

Reset your streak — your previous days are saved. Many people need several attempts. The trend matters more than any single day.

Is sugar really addictive?

Research shows sugar activates the same brain reward pathways as addictive substances. It triggers dopamine release and can create dependency patterns.
