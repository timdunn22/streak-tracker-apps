Frequently Asked Questions

Is IcePlunge really free?

Yes. No subscription, no equipment sales, no upsells. Just a free tracker.

Do cold showers count?

Absolutely. Any deliberate cold exposure counts — cold showers, ice baths, cold plunge tubs, lake swims. Track whatever you do.

How long should I plunge?

Start with 30 seconds to 1 minute. Work up to 2-5 minutes over weeks. Even 30 seconds triggers beneficial norepinephrine release.

What does cold exposure actually do?

Research shows cold exposure increases norepinephrine (2-3x), elevates dopamine (250%+), activates brown fat, reduces inflammation, and improves recovery.

I skipped a day. Reset?

Your choice. Some people track consecutive daily plunges strictly, others allow rest days. IcePlunge saves your history either way.
